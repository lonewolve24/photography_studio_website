Who I Am

I'm Alex, a professional photographer with over 15 years of experience in capturing life's most beautiful moments. My journey began when I was gifted my first camera at the age of 12, and I've been passionate about photography ever since.

What started as a hobby quickly developed into a full-fledged career. After studying photography at the New York Institute of Fine Arts, I worked with several renowned photographers before establishing my own studio in 2010.

My approach to photography is deeply personal. I believe every photoshoot is a collaboration between myself and my clients, aiming to tell authentic stories through compelling imagery that will stand the test of time.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking in nature, exploring new destinations, or spending time with my family and golden retriever, Max.
